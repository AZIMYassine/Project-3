Project discription

This Project is going to solve the problem for the music producer and artists. This Project takes the information of multiple genre music the information is like ( BPM , Key, Structure and Sound Quality ....) and show the data to the music producer that which BPM , Key or some other characteristic of music will work for the music genre he is going to use.This project will benifit the producer , artist, and other other people related to music industry.This Project will give then the information when ever they need.
